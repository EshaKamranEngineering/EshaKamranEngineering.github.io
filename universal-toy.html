Universal Design Toy

A modular toy car designed for kids ages 3–9 with interchangeable parts, rolling wheels, and a simple, intuitive design.

Design Focus

Designed for young users, this toy emphasizes simplicity, durability, and ease of interaction. Large features and smooth geometry incorporating magnet holes make the toy easy to handle, while the modular structure allows parts to be assembled and reconfigured without tools.

The design process included CAD modeling, dimensioned drawings, and prototyping to ensure manufacturability and usability.
